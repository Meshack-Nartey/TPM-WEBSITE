Transformation Project Ministries

Transformation Project Ministries (TPM) is a Christ-centered, Spirit-led ministry with a clear mandate to transform lives through the saving knowledge of Jesus Christ. We exist to raise men and women who are rooted in God's Word, empowered by the Holy Spirit, and committed to fulfilling their divine purpose.

TPM is more than a church — it is a family, a training ground, and a movement.

Our Mandate

  • Winning souls and establishing them in Christ
  • Raising faithful workers, leaders, and ministers
  • Teaching believers to serve God with excellence, loyalty, and devotion
  • Building strong church families that grow spiritually and numerically
  • Equipping the next generation to walk in purpose and power

Our Ministry Culture

TPM is built on love, humility, loyalty, diligence, and excellence. We believe that every believer has a place and a purpose in God's house. Our ministry emphasizes active service, accountability, and spiritual growth — creating an environment where people are nurtured, trained, and released to serve effectively.

What We Believe

  • The saving power of Jesus Christ
  • The transforming work of the Holy Spirit
  • The authority of God's Word
  • Every believer having a purpose in God's house

Our teachings are practical, Bible-based, and designed to help you live out your faith daily.

Our Mission

Our mission is to transform lives, raise disciples, and equip believers to serve God wholeheartedly and advance His Kingdom.

  • Winning souls and establishing them in Christ
  • Training and raising faithful workers and leaders
  • Building strong Christian character rooted in love, humility, and loyalty
  • Helping believers live fruitful and impactful lives

Our Gatherings & Programs

At Transformation Project Ministries (TPM), our gatherings are designed to create spaces where people encounter God, grow in faith, and are empowered for Kingdom advancement. Through our regular services, leadership trainings, and workers' camps—including CLAP Breakfast Meeting, CLAW, Sharpening Camp, Mark 10:29–30 Camp, Mighty Camp, and Metamorphoo Camp—believers are trained, strengthened, and equipped for effective service.

In addition, our special gatherings such as the Holy Ghost Festival, TPM Waits, Transformation Conference, Transformation Experience, and Prophetic Prayer Move are powerful moments of spiritual renewal. In these meetings, lives are saved, revived, healed, and stirred with fresh passion for God.

All our gatherings are marked by the tangible presence of God, sound and practical teaching, fervent prayer, and prophetic insight, creating an atmosphere where genuine transformation takes place.

Our Heart for Compassion

At Transformation Project Ministries (TPM), transformation goes beyond the walls of the church. We believe the love of Christ must be lived out in practical ways that touch lives and restore hope.

Through Transformed Foundations, our compassion and outreach arm, we actively support orphanages and vulnerable children by providing care, resources, and encouragement. This initiative reflects our deep conviction that every child deserves love, dignity, and the opportunity to thrive.

Transformed Foundations is our response to God's call to care for the fatherless and demonstrate His love through action. Through donations, visits, prayer, and acts of service, we extend the heart of Christ to those in need—showing them that they are seen, valued, and loved.

As a church family, we joyfully partner together to make a difference, believing that when lives are touched with love, true transformation begins.

"Religion that God our Father accepts as pure and faultless is this: to look after orphans and widows in their distress." — James 1:27 NIV

The Founder

Apostle Andrews Amoh Ofori

Founder & Executive President – Transformation Project Ministries (TPM)

Apostle Andrews Amoh Ofori is the Founder and Executive President of Transformation Project Ministries (TPM), a vibrant Christian ministry with a divine mandate to transform lives through Christ Jesus. His life and ministry are driven by a deep passion to see people encounter Christ, grow in faith, and walk boldly in their God-given purpose.

Ministry & Calling

A diligent and devoted minister of the Gospel, Apostle Andrews A. Ofori serves through preaching, teaching, prophetic ministry, and healing. His ministry is marked by strong biblical foundations, spiritual depth, wisdom, and uncompromising excellence. Through his ministrations, many lives have experienced genuine transformation, lasting encounters, and remarkable testimonies—both locally and internationally.

Author & Teacher

He is the author of Daily Drops of Transformation: A Daily Devotional to Transform Your Life, a powerful devotional designed to provide daily spiritual nourishment and practical insights from God's Word.

In addition to this, he has written many other life-changing and transformational books, including Crossing the Red Sea, The Fruitful Christian, The Wise and Trusted Servant, God Is Good, No Delay, Are You That Someone That Will Serve the Lord from Your Heart, The Faithful Christian, The Revived Christian, Do Your Very Best for God, Fruitfulness, and many more. Through these writings, he continues to equip believers with biblical truth, spiritual wisdom, and practical guidance for victorious Christian living.

Leadership & Affiliation

Apostle Andrews A. Ofori is a member of Young Ministers' Network International (YMNI), a growing fellowship of young Gospel ministers under the leadership of the renowned Teaching Priest, Rev. John Winfred.

Passion for the Kingdom

A strong proponent of every believer actively serving the Lord, he exemplifies his convictions through selfless service to God. His passion for soul-winning, commitment to ministry, humility of heart, and burning desire to see God's purposes fulfilled have resulted in countless miracles and life-changing encounters. His favorite declaration, "It is a great blessing," reflects his life of gratitude and faith.

Church Growth & Leadership Development

He firmly believes in Church Leaders and Workers Conferences and Camps as strategic platforms for raising effective leaders and strengthening the Church for both qualitative and quantitative growth. Through practical, dynamic, and Spirit-filled teaching, divine keys for church growth and multiplication are released.

Signature Program

Apostle Andrews A. Ofori hosts the annual Holy Ghost Festival, a powerful, spirit-filled gathering where many lives are saved, empowered, and ignited with fresh passion for advancing the Kingdom of God.

Raising the Next Generation

With a strong burden for the future of the Church, he is deeply committed to raising young ministers and leaders who will labor tirelessly for the Kingdom of Heaven. His bold faith and unconventional approach continue to challenge and inspire many young people to go all out for God. His life and ministry testify to this truth: it is a great blessing to serve the Lord.
