Partner with Us Today

At Transformation Project Ministries, we believe in the power of partnership. Whether through financial giving, volunteering your time, or interceding in prayer, every partner plays a vital role in fulfilling our mission to transform lives through the Gospel.

Financial Partnership

Support our ministries through financial giving. Your generosity enables us to reach more people and expand God's Kingdom.

Time & Service

Volunteer your skills and talents. We have opportunities for everyone to serve and make a difference.

Prayer Partnership

Intercede for our ministries, leaders, and the people we serve. Prayer is the foundation of all we do.

Sponsorship

Sponsor children, missionaries, or community projects. Make a direct impact on lives and families.

Mission Trips

Experience missions firsthand by joining our annual trips to serve communities around the world.

Advocacy & Promotion

Share our message with your network. Word-of-mouth is one of the most powerful tools for ministry growth.
