Worker Groups

Every member of our church body has gifts and talents. Discover where you can serve and make a meaningful impact in God's Kingdom.

Communion Stewards

Serve with reverence in preparing and administering Holy Communion.

Ushering

Welcome members and guests, manage seating, and support service flow.

Protocol

Assist with order, coordination, and hospitality for special services.

Hospitality and Welfare

Care for members and guests with warmth, support, and compassion.

Pure Word

Support teaching, discipleship, and scriptural study ministries.

Media and Publicity

Handle visuals, announcements, and ministry communications.

Music

Lead worship through instruments, vocals, and choir ministry.

Theatre and Arts

Use drama, dance, and creative arts to communicate the Gospel.

Finance

Steward the ministry's finances with integrity and accountability.

Organizing

Plan services, schedules, and church events for smooth execution.

Sounds and Technical

Manage audio, lighting, and technical setups for services.

Growth

Encourage spiritual growth and integration of new members.

Literature

Distribute books and resources that build faith and understanding.

Miscellaneous

Support as needed across multiple teams and special projects.

The Pastor's Office

Provide administrative and pastoral support to leadership.
